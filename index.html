About me

AverMax is a young and promising Progressive producer and DJ from Minsk.

Like many people, I began to show interest in music when I was still a child, listening to the works of famous artists. Already at the age of 17, he signed his first 2 debut releases on the English label Crossair Recordings [Crossworld Rec.], which received support in various compilations.

After some time, the list of releases includes a work called "Open Gate", remixes and the original version of which received positive reviews from famous producers, as well as regular support. The release was released on Proton Music's sub-label, Eternal Music.

Throughout the entire time, AverMax has been working in parallel on its own radio show called "the Progressive Universe", which won the hearts of many colleagues and listeners with a great sense of music.

I had the honor to perform on the same stage with Swanky Tunes, Fonarev, Bobina, DJ Feel, DJ Magnit.

AverMax is also one of the founders of the well-known Belarusian club radio station Radio One, which has already ended its existence.

Participant of the festivals: "Energy of summer 2012" and "Energy of summer 2013" in Minsk. Supports: MaRLo, DJ Feel.
